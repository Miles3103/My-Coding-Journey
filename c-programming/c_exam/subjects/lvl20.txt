=== LEVEL 20 - TYPE CONVERSION [3/3] : ASCII & Char Math ===
FILE: rendu/lvl20/task.c

  1. Convert uppercase A, M, Z to lowercase by adding 32:
       A -> a
       M -> m
       Z -> z
  2. Convert char digits to int values:
       '5' -> 5
       '9' -> 9

Expected output:
  A -> a
  M -> m
  Z -> z
  '5' -> 5
  '9' -> 9

Tip: 'A'=65, 'a'=97. '0'=48. Digit value = c - '0'.
